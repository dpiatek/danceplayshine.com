Dance Play Shine ☀ provides fun and highly energetic after school activities to Primary schools in South London.

We specialise in dance, drama and singing.

♫ CLASSES

Our fun and highly energetic classes help develop key social skills such as confidence, concentration, co-operation and creative problem solving. Children can choose among wide range of disciplines such as street dance, mixed dance, ballet, glee club (musical theatre), cheerleading, drama and and our new dynamic Spanish language class Spanish Fun/Drama.

We are providing activities for breakfast clubs and after school clubs.

Glee club

Glee club is one of the most popular activity in Dance Play Shine. Children absolutely love it as it combines drama and favourite pop songs with energetic dance routines. The huge success of the US hit TV show GLEE has firmly put the idea of the Glee Club back in the public eye and ear and made it a household name. One of the meaning of the word GLEE describes a feeling of intense delight and joy. And that is exactly what we are aiming for when singing 'Don't stop believin'.

Mixed dance

Mixed dance is a blend of various styles such as: hip-hop, african, bollywood, ballet and contemporary. During the classes children have an opportunity to get to know different music styles, develop a concentration and a spacious awareness. The classes consist of warm-up, stretching, travelling section and working on dance routine, usually performed at the end of the term.

Cheerleading

Cheerleading is an intense physical activity based upon organized routines, usually ranging anywhere from one to three minutes, which contains many components of dance, jumps and cheers. In this highly energetic class we are using action games, gymnastics and dancing with pom-poms.

Street dance

Street dance is a very popular style of dance among young people. It evolved outside of dance studios in any available open space such as streets, dance parties, school yards and parks. In the class children will learn basic technique, some b-boy tricks and will work on the routine, usually performed at the end of the term.

Drama classes

Drama classes is a variety of fun theatre-related activities, including drama, movement and improvisation. In the class children will have opportunity to use their creative and imaginative side, learn some of the acting skills and work towards their final performance.

Ballet classes

Ballet classes through famous ballet stories such as: 'Sleeping beauty' or 'Nutcracker' introduce children to the basic technique, develop their coordination, rhythm, positioning, space awareness, focus and creativity.

Spanish fun/Drama classes

Spanish fun/Drama classes the best way to learn a new language is through fun and interactive activities such as dance, songs, drama games and craft. By playing out the everyday situations children quicker memorise new words and become more confident. They also learn something about Spanish and South American culture. Usually, at the end of the term there will be a short performance.

✍ ABOUT

Dance Play Shine was created by Justyna Ziarek, an experienced dance teacher and choreographer. She draws inspiration from various styles of dance such as African, Bollywood, Jazz, Contemporary, Hip-Hop, Ballet, Yoga and Musical Theatre. She is also a movement director of London's physical theatre company 'Theatre Libre' www.theatrelibre.co.uk
