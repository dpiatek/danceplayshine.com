Dance Play Shine ☀ provides fun and highly energetic after school activities to Primary schools in South London.

We specialise in dance, drama and singing.

♫ CLASSES

Our fun and highly energetic classes help develop key social skills such as confidence, concentration, co-operation and creative problem solving. Children can choose among wide range of disciplines such as street dance, mixed dance, bollywood, zumba, glee club (musical theatre), yoga, cheerleading, drama and martial arts.

We can provide activities for breakfast clubs and after school clubs.

✍ ABOUT

Dance Play Shine was created by Justyna Ziarek, an experienced dance teacher and choreographer. She draws inspiration from various styles of dance such as African, Bollywood, Jazz, Contemporary, Hip-Hop, Ballet, Yoga and Musical Theatre. She is also a movement director of London's physical theatre company 'Theatre Libre'.
